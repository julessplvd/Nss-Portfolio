ABOUT ME

Education

I have always been naturally curious about the inner workings of things, wether natural or man made. I have also always been drawn to creating and art. As I got into college, I felt as if I had to choose between art and science. After a couple of years taking all the biology classes I could, I found myself really missing getting to create. After all, I can always pick up a book and learn, but there’s something so exciting to be able to make something that didn’t exist before.

Interests

One of my teachers suggested that I take the new Interactive Media class that was being offered the next semester. On the first day my teacher showed us a video of BOX which demonstrated what could be made when you combine projection mapping, software and robotics. There was also a touring exhibit called Illusions at the local science museum that displayed the power of art and science to create optical illusions. I was amazed and hooked and could’t wait to know more. I learned very quickly that i’d need to learn to code if I wanted to do anything like that.

Experience

I started taking tutorials online in my free time, and as time went on, I realized have a passion for technology and software development. Last spring I had the opportunity to attend Nashville Software School where I got to write code, learn how to be a beneficial part of a development team and be mentored for 35 hours a week. I'm excited to turn my passion into a career!
